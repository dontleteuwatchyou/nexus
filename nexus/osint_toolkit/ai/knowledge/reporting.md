# Evidence and reporting

Un rapport doit contenir une preuve reproductible pour chaque constat :
cible affectée, source, date, requête ou commande pertinente, résultat,
niveau de confiance, impact et correction recommandée.

Each finding needs a title, affected asset, source, timestamp, evidence,
confidence, impact and remediation. Remove secrets and unnecessary personal
data before sharing a report. Separate raw observations, analyst inference and
unverified leads.

Use severity only after considering exploitability and business impact.
Deduplicate findings that share the same root cause. State limitations,
timeouts and unavailable sources. A useful executive summary says what was
tested, what matters most and what should happen next.
